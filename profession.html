Apple

Apple is a consumer electronics company, infamous for their sleak designs in both the hardware and software departments.

Apple's culture

Apple's culture is said to be an organizational culture for creative innovation. Apple strives for excellence and creativeness while also being secretive about what they have in the works.

Google

An American multinational technology company, specialize in internet-related services, online advertising technologies, search engine, cloud computing, software and hardware, consumer electronics

Google's culture

Google strives for a culture that is accepting of people no matter their background. They care about qualifications and what a person can contribute to their community. There is also a hands-on culture at Google as they believe learning is best done by experimenting.

IT Jobs

    Staff Software Engineer
    Privacy Engineer
    Data Science Lead
    Data Center Technician
    Enterprise Cloud Architect

Facebook

Facebook is a social media conglomerate and social networking service connecting millions of people in virtual communities world-wide.

Facebook's culture

The culture at Facebook is said to be focused on creative problem solving and decision making. There is also a focus on clear communication between employees and different departments.

Intel

Intel is deemed to be the largest and most valued semiconductor chip manufacturer world-wide.

Intel's culture

Intel's culture is founded in their customer orientation. They want to put the customer first and make sure they're satisfied. Being a risk-taker is also a value that's incorporated into Intel's culture.

    Robotics HW Engineer
    QA AI Engineer
    System Security Architect
    Deep Learning Software Engineer

Netflix

Netflix is a production company that provides a media service and was founded in 1997. Netflix is known world-wide for it's streaming services which entails a library of various TV shows and movies.

Netflix's culture

The culture at Netflix is based on innovation, courage, and curiosity. The CEO is proud with the treatment of employees that was set at the very beginning of Netflix's launch.
